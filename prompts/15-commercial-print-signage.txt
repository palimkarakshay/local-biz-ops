BUSINESS-SPECIFIC OPS — COMMERCIAL PRINT / SIGNAGE / PROMO
================================================================
WHAT "AFTER THE SITE" MEANS
The site shows products with turnaround + material facts and captures RFQs with
lightweight artwork handling. The job has a distinctive middle step — PROOF
APPROVAL — that gates production. The ops job is to quote the RFQ, get artwork +
a signed-off proof, produce, deliver, then win the reorder (repeat B2B revenue).

LEAD INTENTS (intents[])
- "rfq" (P1) — request for quote (may include artwork).
- "rush" (P1) — time-critical job.
- "reorder" (P2) — repeat of a past job.
- "general" (P3).

CRM PIPELINE (stages[])
rfq -> quote -> proof -> production -> delivered -> won / lost.

FOLLOW-UP (followUp)
Fire immediately. Acknowledge the RFQ, confirm spec/quantity/turnaround, and
give the artwork-submission instructions (accepted formats, where to send large
files). A distinct PROOF-APPROVAL request when the proof is ready — production
doesn't start until it's signed off. delayHours: 0.

REVIEW REQUEST (reviewRequest)
trigger: "job-complete" mapped to DELIVERED. Ask once the client has the goods
in hand. Platform: GOOGLE_REVIEW_URL. No incentives.

INVOICING / REMINDERS (invoice)
Deposit on approval (especially custom/large runs) + balance on delivery, with
reminders on the balance. Repurpose for REORDER reminders keyed off the original
job date (consumables / seasonal signage).

COMPLIANCE FOR OUTBOUND
Truthful capability / turnaround claims only — never over-promise a press or
finish you don't run. Respect client artwork/IP. CASL consent + unsubscribe.

VERTICAL AUTOMATIONS (optional n8n additions)
- A proof-approval reminder loop (nudge until approved) keyed off the proof-sent
  date, so jobs don't stall.
- A reorder-reminder pass in workflow (c) keyed off past delivery dates.
