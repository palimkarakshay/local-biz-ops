BUSINESS-SPECIFIC OPS — PROFESSIONAL SERVICES (vertical selectable)
================================================================
WHAT "AFTER THE SITE" MEANS
The site presents practice areas / services and captures consultation requests
and gated-guide downloads. These leads need a careful, confidential intake — the
follow-up itself must not create a professional relationship or invite
privileged details by open email. The ops job is to book the consult, run a
compliant intake, then bill cleanly and (where the regulator permits) earn a
review.

LEAD INTENTS (intents[])
- "consultation" (P1) — wants to book time.
- "intake" (P2) — ready to engage; route to secure intake.
- "quote" (P2) — fee/scope question.
- "general" (P3).

CRM PIPELINE (stages[])
inquiry -> consult-booked -> engaged -> matter-active -> matter-complete -> won / lost.

FOLLOW-UP (followUp)
Fire immediately. Confirm the consult slot and STATE PLAINLY that no
lawyer-client / advisor relationship is formed by the email and that they should
NOT send confidential or privileged details by email — link to a secure intake
instead. Keep "results may vary" where outcomes are implied. delayHours: 0.

REVIEW REQUEST (reviewRequest)
trigger: "job-complete" mapped to MATTER-COMPLETE. IMPORTANT: LSO (law) and CPA
Ontario advertising rules constrain testimonials and outcome claims — ask only
for a factual Google review of the service experience, never a case-outcome
endorsement. Platform: GOOGLE_REVIEW_URL. If unsure, leave the review step OFF
and flag it for the professional to approve.

INVOICING / REMINDERS (invoice)
Retainer request up front + interim/final billing. reminderDays on outstanding
invoices, worded professionally. Keep trust-account handling out of automation.

COMPLIANCE FOR OUTBOUND
LSO (law) or CPA Ontario number in the signature (TODO until provided). "No
relationship via this form/email" + "results may vary" disclaimers. Privilege /
confidentiality respected — secure channel for anything sensitive. CASL.

VERTICAL AUTOMATIONS (optional n8n additions)
- Gate the review step behind a manual approval (a label or admin click) given
  the advertising rules.
- Route "intake" leads to a secure-intake link rather than the standard
  follow-up in workflow (a).
