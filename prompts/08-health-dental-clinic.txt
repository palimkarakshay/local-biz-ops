BUSINESS-SPECIFIC OPS — HEALTH / DENTAL CLINIC (vertical selectable)
================================================================
WHAT "AFTER THE SITE" MEANS
The site lists services + clinicians (with College registration) and captures
new-patient and booking requests. These leads carry health information, so the
intake and every follow-up are PHIPA-sensitive — keep personal health info OUT
of email and link to a secure form/portal. The ops job is to book the visit,
keep the chair full with recalls, and (within College rules) earn reviews.

LEAD INTENTS (intents[])
- "new-patient" (P1) — accepting-new-patients inquiry; respond fast.
- "booking" (P1) — appointment request.
- "treatment" (P2) — specific treatment question.
- "general" (P3).

CRM PIPELINE (stages[])
inquiry -> booked -> seen -> recall-due -> won / lost.

FOLLOW-UP (followUp)
Fire immediately. Confirm the appointment basics and link to a SECURE intake for
medical history — NEVER collect or echo health details in email. State the
accepting-new-patients status. delayHours: 0.

REVIEW REQUEST (reviewRequest)
trigger: "job-complete" mapped to SEEN (post-visit). College advertising rules
(RCDSO / CPSO / Physio / CCO) restrict testimonials and outcome claims — ask
only for a factual Google review of the experience, never a clinical-outcome
endorsement. Platform: GOOGLE_REVIEW_URL. Gate behind clinician approval if
unsure.

INVOICING / REMINDERS (invoice)
Patient balance after insurance (reminderDays on the balance). Repurpose for
RECALL reminders — 6-month hygiene / follow-up — keyed off the last visit date.

COMPLIANCE FOR OUTBOUND
PHIPA: minimal PHI, secure channel, explicit consent for email communication.
Clinician College registration in the signature (TODO until provided). College
advertising rules on any review/testimonial ask. CASL.

VERTICAL AUTOMATIONS (optional n8n additions)
- Repurpose workflow (c) as a recall scheduler (6-month / annual) off last-visit
  dates instead of overdue invoices.
- An appointment-reminder touch (24-48h before) keyed off the booked slot.
