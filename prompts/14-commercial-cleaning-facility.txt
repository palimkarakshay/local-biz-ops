BUSINESS-SPECIFIC OPS — COMMERCIAL CLEANING / JANITORIAL
================================================================
WHAT "AFTER THE SITE" MEANS
The site presents services by facility type (offices / medical / industrial) and
captures request-a-walkthrough quote leads. These are B2B recurring contracts:
the sale is a walkthrough -> proposal -> signed recurring service. The ops job is
to schedule the walkthrough fast, win the contract, then run reliable recurring
invoicing and protect the relationship with a check-in review.

LEAD INTENTS (intents[])
- "walkthrough" (P1) — wants an on-site quote; book it fast.
- "quote" (P2) — ballpark pricing question.
- "facility-type" (P2) — specialised (medical / industrial) inquiry.
- "general" (P3).

CRM PIPELINE (stages[])
inquiry -> walkthrough -> proposal -> won -> servicing -> lost.

FOLLOW-UP (followUp)
Fire immediately. Confirm the walkthrough window + site contact + what you'll
assess, and lead with insured / WSIB / bonded trust signals. delayHours: 0.

REVIEW REQUEST (reviewRequest)
trigger: "job-complete" mapped to the FIRST MONTH of service (not day one) —
ask the facility manager once they've seen consistent quality. Platform:
GOOGLE_REVIEW_URL. No incentives.

INVOICING / REMINDERS (invoice)
Recurring MONTHLY invoicing with reminders on outstanding balances. Optionally a
quarterly service-review touch. reminderDays apply to each monthly invoice.

COMPLIANCE FOR OUTBOUND
WSIB clearance, liability insurance, bonded status, and WHMIS competence stated
where claimed (TODO until provided) — never invent. CASL consent + unsubscribe.

VERTICAL AUTOMATIONS (optional n8n additions)
- Drive workflow (c) off the recurring monthly billing cycle per contract.
- A quarterly QA / satisfaction check-in touch keyed off the contract start.
