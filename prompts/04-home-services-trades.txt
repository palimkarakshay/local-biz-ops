================================================================
BUSINESS-SPECIFIC OPS — HOME SERVICES / TRADES
================================================================
WHAT "AFTER THE SITE" MEANS
The site (HVAC / plumbing / electrical / landscaping / cleaning / reno — set in
the site's config) captures high-intent local leads who want to call NOW or get
a fast quote. Speed-to-lead and visible trust win the job. After the job, a
fresh Google review is the single biggest driver of the next lead, and getting
paid on time is the difference between a good month and a bad one.

LEAD INTENTS (intents[])  — ships as the default
- "emergency" (P1) — no-heat / no-power / flooding / gas-smell; phone-first.
- "quote" (P2) — wants an estimate.
- "booking" (P2) — wants a service window.
- "general" (P3).

CRM PIPELINE (stages[])  — ships as the default
new -> contacted -> scheduled -> job-complete -> won / lost.

FOLLOW-UP (followUp)  — fires on intake, delayHours: 0
Confirm receipt, set the expectation of a real reply within the hour, and offer
the click-to-call number for emergencies. Vertical-aware tone (furnace/AC/drain/
panel). This is the default template shipped in ops-config.ts.

REVIEW REQUEST (reviewRequest)  — trigger "job-complete", delayDays 1
Marking the job complete fires the Google Business Profile review request (after
a 1-day delay in workflow b). "If we did right by you, a quick review helps your
neighbours" + a "reply first if anything fell short" safety valve. No incentives.

INVOICING / REMINDERS (invoice)  — reminderDays [3, 7, 14]
Deposit on booking (larger jobs) + balance on completion. Escalating but polite
reminders at 3 / 7 / 14 days overdue. Ships as the default.

COMPLIANCE FOR OUTBOUND
Relevant licence in the signature (TSSA / ECRA-ESA / master plumber — TODO until
provided), plus insurance + WSIB clearance and warranty terms where stated.
NEVER invent a licence number. CASL consent + unsubscribe.

VERTICAL AUTOMATIONS (optional n8n additions)
- Switch in workflow (a) routing "emergency" to immediate SMS (Twilio) on top of
  the email follow-up — speed-to-lead for after-hours.
- Seasonal touch: a scheduled "furnace tune-up / AC check / spring cleanup"
  nudge to past job-complete customers (a second use of workflow c's schedule).
