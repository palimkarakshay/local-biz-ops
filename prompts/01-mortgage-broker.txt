BUSINESS-SPECIFIC OPS — MORTGAGE / INSURANCE BROKER
================================================================
WHAT "AFTER THE SITE" MEANS
The site captures rate-shoppers and pre-approval requests. These leads are
rate-sensitive and time-sensitive: speed-to-lead wins the deal. The ops job is
to triage fast, move the file from inquiry to funded, then keep the client for
the renewal years later. Most income is lender-paid, so there is usually no
client invoice — repurpose the invoice workflow as a maturity/renewal reminder.

LEAD INTENTS (intents[])
- "pre-approval" (P1) — actively buying; respond within the hour.
- "rate-inquiry" (P2) — shopping rates; nurture.
- "refinance" (P2) — equity take-out / consolidation.
- "renewal" (P2) — existing mortgage maturing.
- "general" (P3).

CRM PIPELINE (stages[])
new -> qualifying -> application -> approved -> funded -> won / lost.
("funded" is the job-complete equivalent that fires the review request.)

FOLLOW-UP (followUp)
Fire immediately. Confirm receipt, set the expectation of a quick call, and link
to a SECURE document upload (not email) for income/ID docs. NEVER ask for SIN or
banking details by email — link to the secure portal. Include the FSRA-required
"rates subject to change / O.A.C." line. delayHours: 0.

REVIEW REQUEST (reviewRequest)
trigger: "job-complete" mapped to the FUNDED stage. Ask a day or two after
funding/closing, when the client is happiest. Platform: GOOGLE_REVIEW_URL
(Google Business Profile). Keep the ask compliant — no incentives for reviews.

INVOICING / REMINDERS (invoice)
Repurpose as RENEWAL / MATURITY reminders: reminderDays measured before the
maturity date (e.g. 120 / 90 / 30 days out) rather than overdue. Add an annual
"mortgage check-in" touch. Template tone: helpful, not salesy.

COMPLIANCE FOR OUTBOUND
FSRA brokering licence # + agent designation in the signature (TODO tokens until
provided). "Rates subject to change / O.A.C." on rate-bearing emails. PIPEDA:
collect only what's needed; secure channel for sensitive docs. CASL consent +
unsubscribe on every send.

VERTICAL AUTOMATIONS (optional n8n additions)
- Add a Switch on intent in workflow (a) to route "pre-approval" to an SMS
  speed-to-lead nudge (Twilio) in addition to the email follow-up.
- Repoint workflow (c) at a "maturity-dates" view so it nudges renewals N days
  BEFORE maturity instead of N days AFTER an invoice due date.
