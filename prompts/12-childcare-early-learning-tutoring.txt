BUSINESS-SPECIFIC OPS — CHILDCARE / EARLY LEARNING / TUTORING (vertical selectable)
================================================================
WHAT "AFTER THE SITE" MEANS
The site shows programs by age group (with ratios) and captures tour, waitlist,
and enrollment requests. Parents are choosing who to trust with their child, so
the intake handles MINIMAL child data and the tone is reassuring. The ops job is
to book the tour, manage the waitlist transparently, convert to enrollment, then
keep tuition current.

LEAD INTENTS (intents[])
- "tour" (P1) — wants to visit.
- "waitlist" (P1) — wants a spot.
- "enrollment" (P2) — ready to enroll.
- "general" (P3).

CRM PIPELINE (stages[])
inquiry -> tour-booked -> waitlist -> enrolled -> active -> withdrawn.

FOLLOW-UP (followUp)
Fire immediately. Confirm the tour, explain the waitlist position/process
honestly, and link a secure enrollment form. Collect the MINIMUM child data;
never put a child's details in an email body — link to a secure form. State
RECE credentials + that staff hold Vulnerable Sector Checks. delayHours: 0.

REVIEW REQUEST (reviewRequest)
trigger: "job-complete" mapped to a settled-in point (a few weeks after
enrollment) or end of a term. Ask parents for a factual Google review; keep it
gentle and non-pressuring. Platform: GOOGLE_REVIEW_URL.

INVOICING / REMINDERS (invoice)
Recurring TUITION reminders before each cycle (CWELCC-aware where applicable),
plus a gentle overdue nudge. reminderDays map to the tuition cycle.

COMPLIANCE FOR OUTBOUND
CCEYA + Ministry licensing; CWELCC where applicable; staff RECE credentials +
Vulnerable Sector Checks referenced (TODO until provided). MINIMAL child data by
email — secure channel for anything sensitive. CASL consent + unsubscribe.

VERTICAL AUTOMATIONS (optional n8n additions)
- Repurpose workflow (c) as a recurring tuition schedule.
- A waitlist-status update touch (monthly) so families aren't left guessing.
