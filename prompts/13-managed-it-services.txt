================================================================
BUSINESS-SPECIFIC OPS — MANAGED IT SERVICES (MSP, sells to local SMBs)
================================================================
WHAT "AFTER THE SITE" MEANS
The site presents IT services + managed-plan tiers and captures free-assessment
and discovery-call leads, with a sales-vs-support split. The ops job is to route
support away from sales fast, run discovery to a signed managed-services
agreement, onboard cleanly, then keep monthly billing and renewals on track —
recurring revenue is the whole game.

LEAD INTENTS (intents[])
- "support" (P1) — existing client needs help; route to the support/ticket
  inbox, NOT the sales follow-up.
- "assessment" (P1) — wants the free IT assessment.
- "discovery" (P2) — sales discovery call.
- "general" (P3).

CRM PIPELINE (stages[])
inquiry -> discovery -> proposal -> onboarding -> client -> churned.

FOLLOW-UP (followUp)
Fire immediately. For sales intents, confirm + book the assessment/discovery and
set a security-conscious tone. For "support", acknowledge + route to the ticket
system with an SLA expectation — do not run the sales sequence. delayHours: 0.

REVIEW REQUEST (reviewRequest)
trigger: "job-complete" mapped to ONBOARDING complete (and again after a strong
QBR). B2B reviews live on Google + Clutch; ask the champion. Platform:
GOOGLE_REVIEW_URL (+ optional Clutch link). No incentives; truthful claims only.

INVOICING / REMINDERS (invoice)
Monthly managed-plan billing reminders + a RENEWAL reminder ahead of the
agreement anniversary. reminderDays on monthly invoices; add a 60/30-day renewal
nudge.

COMPLIANCE FOR OUTBOUND
PIPEDA on client data. Truthful certification/partner claims only (no invented
badges). SLA terms accurate. CASL consent + unsubscribe.

VERTICAL AUTOMATIONS (optional n8n additions)
- Switch in workflow (a) routing "support" to the ticketing inbox vs sales
  intents to the discovery sequence.
- A renewal-anniversary nudge in workflow (c) (N days before) alongside monthly
  billing reminders.
