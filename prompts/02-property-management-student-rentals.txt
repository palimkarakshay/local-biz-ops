BUSINESS-SPECIFIC OPS — PROPERTY MANAGEMENT / STUDENT RENTALS
================================================================
WHAT "AFTER THE SITE" MEANS
The site lists units near UW / Laurier / Conestoga and captures viewing and
application requests. Student-rental demand is seasonal and bursty — many leads
per unit in a short window. The ops job is to confirm viewings fast, move
qualified applicants to a signed lease, then keep the tenant relationship warm
and rent paid on time.

LEAD INTENTS (intents[])
- "viewing" (P1) — wants to see a unit; book fast or lose them.
- "application" (P2) — ready to apply.
- "maintenance" (P2) — existing tenant; route to a maintenance inbox.
- "general" (P3).

CRM PIPELINE (stages[])
inquiry -> viewing-booked -> application -> lease-signed -> tenant -> moved-out.
(lease-signed / move-in is the job-complete equivalent for the review step.)

FOLLOW-UP (followUp)
Fire immediately. For "viewing", confirm the slot + address + what to bring and
link to the booking page. For "application", link to the online application.
CRITICAL: the application and any follow-up MUST omit Ontario Human Rights Code
prohibited questions (no asking about family status, citizenship, etc.).
Reference the Ontario Standard Lease + RTA. delayHours: 0.

REVIEW REQUEST (reviewRequest)
trigger: "job-complete" mapped to MOVE-IN (a week or two after, once settled),
and again at a smooth lease renewal. Platform: GOOGLE_REVIEW_URL.

INVOICING / REMINDERS (invoice)
Repurpose as RENT reminders: a few days BEFORE the 1st (friendly), and an
escalating LATE-RENT notice after, worded to respect the RTA (no threats; lawful
N4 timelines handled separately). reminderDays apply to the rent due date.

COMPLIANCE FOR OUTBOUND
RTA + Standard Lease references. Human Rights Code: zero prohibited questions in
any templated message. PIPEDA on applicant data. CASL consent + unsubscribe.

VERTICAL AUTOMATIONS (optional n8n additions)
- Repoint workflow (c) at a recurring monthly rent schedule rather than one-off
  invoices; key reminders off each lease's rent-due day.
- Add a maintenance-intake branch in workflow (a) that routes "maintenance"
  leads to a separate tracker/inbox instead of the sales follow-up.
