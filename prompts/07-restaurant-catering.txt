BUSINESS-SPECIFIC OPS — RESTAURANT / CATERING
================================================================
WHAT "AFTER THE SITE" MEANS
The site shows the menu (with dietary tags) + hours and captures catering and
private-event inquiries plus reservation requests. Walk-in dining mostly self-
serves; the ops value is in catering — higher ticket, planned ahead, repeatable.
The ops job is to quote catering fast, lock the date with a deposit, deliver,
and bank the post-event review.

LEAD INTENTS (intents[])
- "catering" (P1) — event catering quote.
- "reservation" (P2) — large party / private booking.
- "event" (P2) — buyout / private event.
- "general" (P3).

CRM PIPELINE (stages[])
inquiry -> quote -> booked -> event-complete -> won / lost.

FOLLOW-UP (followUp)
Fire immediately. For "catering", confirm date / headcount / dietary needs and
send the menu + a quote next-step. ALWAYS include the allergen disclaimer in any
menu-bearing message. delayHours: 0.

REVIEW REQUEST (reviewRequest)
trigger: "job-complete" mapped to EVENT-COMPLETE (catering) or a post-visit ask
for private bookings. Platform: GOOGLE_REVIEW_URL (and optionally a second
dining-review link). No incentives.

INVOICING / REMINDERS (invoice)
Deposit to hold the date + balance due before/at the event. reminderDays on the
balance; don't let it lapse past the event date.

COMPLIANCE FOR OUTBOUND
Allergen disclaimer on every menu/quote email. Keep advertised hours in config
and in sync with Google Business. CASL consent + unsubscribe.

VERTICAL AUTOMATIONS (optional n8n additions)
- A "final headcount + dietary confirmation" touch keyed off the event date
  (reuse the schedule trigger).
- Switch in workflow (a) routing "reservation" to the host/booking inbox vs
  "catering" to the events inbox.
