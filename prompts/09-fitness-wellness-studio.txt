BUSINESS-SPECIFIC OPS — FITNESS / WELLNESS STUDIO (vertical selectable)
================================================================
WHAT "AFTER THE SITE" MEANS
The site shows classes / programs / membership tiers and captures free-trial
requests. The whole funnel is trial -> first visit -> member -> retained. The
ops job is to get the trial booked while motivation is high, convert it to a
membership, then keep dues paid and members from quietly churning.

LEAD INTENTS (intents[])
- "free-trial" (P1) — book the trial fast.
- "membership" (P2) — pricing / sign-up.
- "class-info" (P2) — schedule / class question.
- "general" (P3).

CRM PIPELINE (stages[])
inquiry -> trial-booked -> trial-attended -> member -> at-risk -> churned.

FOLLOW-UP (followUp)
Fire immediately. Confirm the trial slot, link the PAR-Q / waiver to complete
beforehand, and say what to bring. A no-show nudge if the trial passes
un-attended. delayHours: 0.

REVIEW REQUEST (reviewRequest)
trigger: "job-complete" mapped to a few sessions in (not after one trial) — ask
when they're seeing results. Platform: GOOGLE_REVIEW_URL. No incentives.

INVOICING / REMINDERS (invoice)
Recurring membership dues: reminder before each cycle + a DUNNING sequence on a
failed payment (retry tone, then a "let's fix your card" nudge). reminderDays
map to the billing cycle.

COMPLIANCE FOR OUTBOUND
Ontario Consumer Protection rules on membership terms / cancellation referenced.
PAR-Q / waiver before activity. No medical or outcome guarantees. CASL consent +
unsubscribe.

VERTICAL AUTOMATIONS (optional n8n additions)
- Repurpose workflow (c) as recurring dues + failed-payment dunning rather than
  one-off invoices.
- An at-risk win-back touch when a member hasn't attended in N days (reuse the
  schedule trigger against attendance data).
