BUSINESS-SPECIFIC OPS — STAFFING / RECRUITING AGENCY (two audiences)
================================================================
WHAT "AFTER THE SITE" MEANS
The site serves TWO audiences: employers (request talent — the primary revenue
lead) and candidates (submit a resume). They must NOT be mixed: different
inboxes, different follow-ups, different data handling. The ops job is to respond
to employers fast with a talent plan, keep candidates warm, make the placement,
then invoice the fee and earn the employer review.

LEAD INTENTS (intents[])
- "employer-request" (P1) — employer needs talent; PRIMARY lead, route to the
  employer inbox.
- "candidate-resume" (P2) — candidate submission; route to the candidate inbox.
- "general" (P3).

CRM PIPELINE (stages[])
request -> intake -> shortlist -> interview -> placement -> won / lost.
(Track candidates as a separate pool/pipeline, not mixed with employer deals.)

FOLLOW-UP (followUp)
Fire immediately, AUDIENCE-AWARE: employers get a "we're on it + here's the
next step" with a discovery slot; candidates get a respectful acknowledgement +
realistic expectations. Keep the two templates separate. delayHours: 0.

REVIEW REQUEST (reviewRequest)
trigger: "job-complete" mapped to a successful PLACEMENT (past the guarantee
period). Ask the EMPLOYER for a Google / Clutch review; handle candidate
testimonials carefully and only with explicit consent. Platform:
GOOGLE_REVIEW_URL (+ optional Clutch). No incentives.

INVOICING / REMINDERS (invoice)
Placement-fee invoicing on a successful hire (often tied to a guarantee period),
with reminders on outstanding fees. reminderDays on the placement invoice.

COMPLIANCE FOR OUTBOUND
Ontario temp-help / recruiter LICENSING referenced (TODO until provided). Human
Rights Code: zero prohibited fields/questions in any templated message to either
audience. Candidate data privacy (PIPEDA). CASL consent + unsubscribe.

VERTICAL AUTOMATIONS (optional n8n additions)
- Switch in workflow (a) routing "employer-request" vs "candidate-resume" to
  separate inboxes + separate follow-up templates.
- A guarantee-period check-in to the employer before requesting the review.
