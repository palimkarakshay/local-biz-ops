BUSINESS-SPECIFIC OPS — VETERINARY / PET SERVICES
================================================================
WHAT "AFTER THE SITE" MEANS
The site lists services + vet/RVT bios (with CVO licence) and captures new-client
and appointment/grooming requests, with an emergency strip. The ops job is to
get the appointment booked, run a clean new-client intake, then keep pets on
schedule with vaccination + wellness recalls — the recurring heartbeat of the
practice.

LEAD INTENTS (intents[])
- "emergency" (P1) — urgent; phone-first, route immediately.
- "new-client" (P1) — accepting-patients inquiry.
- "appointment" (P2) — wellness / grooming / boarding booking.
- "general" (P3).

CRM PIPELINE (stages[])
inquiry -> booked -> seen -> recall-due -> won / lost.

FOLLOW-UP (followUp)
Fire immediately. Confirm the appointment, link the new-client intake form, note
the emergency line + after-hours guidance. Keep it warm — people love their
pets. delayHours: 0.

REVIEW REQUEST (reviewRequest)
trigger: "job-complete" mapped to SEEN. CVO advertising rules constrain
claims/testimonials — ask for a factual Google review of the experience.
Platform: GOOGLE_REVIEW_URL. No incentives.

INVOICING / REMINDERS (invoice)
Visit balance (reminderDays on the balance). Repurpose for VACCINATION / wellness
RECALLS keyed off due dates — the highest-value recurring touch in the practice.

COMPLIANCE FOR OUTBOUND
CVO licence in the signature (TODO until provided) and CVO advertising rules on
any review ask. Emergency guidance present. CASL consent + unsubscribe.

VERTICAL AUTOMATIONS (optional n8n additions)
- Repurpose workflow (c) as a vaccination/recall scheduler off due dates.
- An appointment-reminder touch (24-48h) keyed off the booked slot.
