================================================================
BUSINESS-SPECIFIC OPS — AUTO DEALERSHIP
================================================================
WHAT "AFTER THE SITE" MEANS
The site shows inventory (VIN/km/Carfax) and captures e-price, test-drive, and
trade-in valuation leads. Buyers shop several dealers at once, so the first
honest, specific reply usually wins. The ops job is to send the e-price fast,
book the test drive, close the deal, then turn the buyer into a repeat
service-and-referral customer.

LEAD INTENTS (intents[])
- "e-price" (P1) — wants the out-the-door price on a specific VIN.
- "test-drive" (P1) — book an appointment.
- "trade-in" (P2) — wants a valuation.
- "finance" (P2) — pre-qualify.
- "general" (P3).

CRM PIPELINE (stages[])
new -> appointment -> test-drive -> deal -> delivered -> won / lost.
(delivered is the job-complete equivalent that fires the review request.)

FOLLOW-UP (followUp)
Fire immediately. For "e-price", quote ALL-IN per OMVIC (price + HST + licensing
disclosed; no hidden fees) and link to the VIN. For "test-drive", confirm the
slot + what to bring (licence). Keep finance pre-qual on a secure channel — no
SIN by email. delayHours: 0.

REVIEW REQUEST (reviewRequest)
trigger: "job-complete" mapped to DELIVERED. Ask at delivery + a day later.
Platform: GOOGLE_REVIEW_URL (and optionally a dealer-review site as a second
link). No incentives for reviews.

INVOICING / REMINDERS (invoice)
Deposit + balance reminders on a pending deal (reminderDays from the agreed
date). Then repurpose for SERVICE reminders (oil change / seasonal tire swap /
warranty milestone) keyed off the delivery date.

COMPLIANCE FOR OUTBOUND
OMVIC registration # in the signature (TODO until provided) and all-in pricing
in any price-bearing email. PIPEDA on finance data; secure channel for sensitive
info. CASL consent + unsubscribe.

VERTICAL AUTOMATIONS (optional n8n additions)
- Switch in workflow (a) routing "e-price" / "test-drive" to immediate SMS as
  well as email (speed-to-lead).
- Second pass of workflow (c) as a seasonal service-reminder schedule.
